About Smart Energy Calculator

Your AI-powered tool for smarter, greener, and more cost-effective electricity usage

What is Smart Energy Calculator?

Smart Energy Calculator is a modern web application that predicts your household's electricity consumption, visualizes appliance-wise usage, and delivers personalized, AI-powered energy-saving recommendations—all based on your real data, not generic tips.

  • Accurate bill and load prediction
  • Beautiful, interactive charts
  • Actionable, appliance-specific AI advice
  • Dark/yellow modern theme for clarity and accessibility

How It Works

1. Enter Your Household & Appliances

Provide your family size and all appliances, including brand, model, and usage hours.

2. Get Instant Predictions

See your monthly bill, load, and appliance-wise breakdown with interactive charts.

3. AI-Powered Recommendations

Receive tailored, actionable tips to save energy and money—no generic advice, only what fits your real usage.

Technologies Used

Built with Flask, Bootstrap, Chart.js, Groq Llama AI, and a custom dark/yellow theme for a seamless, modern experience.

Why Choose This App?

  • Truly personalized: All advice is based on your real appliances, brands, and usage.
  • Transparent: See exactly how your bill and savings are calculated.
  • Modern, accessible design: Works beautifully on any device, day or night.
  • Open and reference-backed: Uses official UP government tariff data and calculators.
